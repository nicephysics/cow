The Trapdoor

As you stand up, you realise that the hard and shiny ‘ground’ you were sleeping on was none other than a metal trapdoor! Recalling the terrible events of the tornado, your first thought was to hide inside it, on the occasion it happens again. You try using your legs to open it, but to no avail.
Suddenly, you spot a number pad in the middle of the trapdoor. Under it was a note:

This is a 4.8 m long trapdoor.
This trapdoor can be opened perpendicularly
with a turning moment of 1200 Nm.
The minimum amount of force (in Newtons) that should be applied to open the trapdoor,
is the code to enter this place.

You rack your brains. This simple question should be a piece of cake to you, you think. After a while, the answer strikes you. Aha! You punch your answer on the pad, and the trapdoor swings open, leading you to another place…
