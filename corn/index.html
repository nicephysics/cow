The Corn

“Crack!” You jump, as you hear a noise amongst the pin-drop silence. You look up and spot something about to drop down from the ceiling. “A… a CORN!” You shout. “MOO, MOO, MOO!” Your shocked voice echoes around the huge cave. Being an inquisitive Physics cow, you think, “Now… what should my average speed be to exactly catch it when it drops?”
You assume that air resistance is negligible in the cave, the ceiling is 10 meters high, and you need to run the shortest distance of 15 meters in order to catch it.
Just when you finish your calculation, the corn drops down, and you run forward (with your calculated speed) to catch it. Yum! Why would a fresh corn be here? You think, as you happily eat the golden corn.

What was your average speed to exactly catch the corn as it dropped?
