Is There Someone Here?

This part of the cave is so big, you think, as you continue to explore the place. You walk around, taking sips of water in small ponds along the way. “It must be getting darker out there,” you moo aloud.
Suddenly, you spot something. Something that looks like a cylinder. Something familiar. You walk closer to it, and realise it is a water bottle - just like the ones you see the humans using in the farm! It seemed pretty new… “Is there someone here?” You think, surprised. The previous corn incident further emphasised this concept. You walk further into the cave, and realise the ceiling is getting lower and lower, and the cave is becoming less oddly-shaped.
Then, you discover a new turn, and a neatly carved-out place greets you. A suspicious door in the centre catches your attention…

You have reached the end! Yay!
